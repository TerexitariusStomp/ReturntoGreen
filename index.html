Return to Green

July 23rd 7pm to July 31st 4pm NZST

One of the first metaverse events for the regenerative finance movement.

A time to connect with one another and empower underrepresented populations that are part of this movement to lead global dialogue.

A chance for a private immersive art event from Christchurch, New Zealand to be experienced by a global community and connect with this movement.
